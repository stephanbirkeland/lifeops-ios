## Design Principles

1. **Autonomy** - Support choices, don't control
2. **No Guilt** - Track without judgment
3. **Positive Framing** - Focus on gains, not losses
4. **Sustainable Change** - Small steps over dramatic shifts
5. **Individual Variation** - What works varies by person
