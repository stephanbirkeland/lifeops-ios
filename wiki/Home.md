# Welcome to LifeOps

LifeOps is a privacy-first, self-hosted personal life management system with gamification.

## What is LifeOps?

LifeOps helps you:
- **Track your daily habits** with streaks and achievements
- **Monitor your health** via Oura Ring integration
- **Gamify your life** with RPG-style stats and skill trees
- **Manage your routines** with smart scheduling
- **Maintain privacy** - all data stays on your own servers

## Philosophy

> Technology should improve life, not consume it.

LifeOps is built on these principles:
1. **Privacy First** - Your data belongs to you
2. **Intentional Technology** - Help you live better
3. **Unified Experience** - One system across all devices
4. **Proactive Assistance** - Suggest, don't nag
