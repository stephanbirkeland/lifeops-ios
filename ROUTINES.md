## Late Night Traps

These activities tend to keep me up too late:

| Trap | Trigger | Mitigation Ideas |
|------|---------|------------------|
| Phone scrolling | Boredom, habit | Screen time limits, grayscale mode |
| Computer/work | Projects, deadlines | Hard stop reminders |
| TV binge | Good shows | Episode limits, "last episode" warning |
| Gaming | Engaging games | Session time tracking |

### LifeOps Intervention Strategies
1. **Gentle reminders** at target bedtime
2. **Screen time tracking** with daily limits
3. **Wind-down mode** - dim lights, suggest sleep
4. **Morning reminder** - "You wanted to wake at 6:00, sleep now for 8 hours"
5. **No guilt** - tracking without judgment
